Tozeur

Tozeur (Arabic: توزر) is a Tunisian city on the borders of the Atlas and the Sahara Desert, the largest of the five oases of the Jerid.
Tozeur has 37 365 inhabitants.
Located north-west of Chott el-Jérid, near the Algerian border, Tozeur is 450 kilometers south-west of Tunis.

Medenine

Medenine (Arabic: مدنين) is a city in the south-eastern part of Tunisia.
Medenine has 71 406 inhabitants
It occupies a central position in the Tunisian south-east because it is located 75 kilometers south of Gabès, 78 kilometers west of Ben Gardane and about fifty kilometers north of Tataouine; the capital Tunis is 482 kilometers to the north.

Kasserine

Kasserine (Arabic: القصرين), is a city located in the center-west of Tunisia.
The population reaches 83,534 inhabitants.
Located at an altitude of more than 656 meters, the city is surrounded by three mountains: the Chambi djebels in the west (highest point in Tunisia at 1,544 meters5), Semmama in the north (1,314 meters6) and Essalloum in the east (1373 meters).
