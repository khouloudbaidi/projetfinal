Tunis

Tunis est la ville la plus peuplée et la capitale de la Tunisie.
The population of the municipality of Tunis is 638,845 inhabitants according to the census of the National Institute of Statistics.

Bizerte

Bizerte or Banzart (Arabic: بنزرت) is a city in northern Tunisia located between the Mediterranean Sea and Lake Bizerte.
The city had 136,917 inhabitants.
It is located about sixty kilometers northwest of Tunis, the country's capital, and five kilometers from Cape Blanc, the northern tip of Africa.

Nabeul

Nabeul (Arabic: نابل) is a city in northeastern Tunisia, located south of the Cape Bon peninsula, about sixty kilometers southeast of Tunis.
It constitutes a municipality with 70,437 inhabitants.
By associating the neighboring towns of Dar Chaâbane, Béni Khiar and El Maâmoura, it forms an agglomeration. With Hammamet, it forms a bipolar conurbation.
